Comparing and Contrasting:

  • When we compare, we look for things that are the same.
  • When we contrast, we find things that are different.
  • Look for special words like "both," "similar," "but," or "however" to help you spot comparisons and differences.
  • This helps us understand and remember information better!

Let us take a look at words used for “compare” and “contrast”:

Words used for comparing Words used for contrasting
Same way But
Both While
Similarly Different
Also Unlike, However, On the other hand
